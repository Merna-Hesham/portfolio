## Overview

Goia is an AI-powered virtual tour guide app designed to revolutionize the tourism experience. As Co-founder and COO, I led the project from ideation to product launch.

## Key Features

- **Computer Vision Artifact Recognition**: Users can point their camera at historical artifacts to receive instant information and stories.
- **AR Prototype**: Integrated Augmented Reality features using Unity to enhance the immersive experience.
- **AI Integration**: Leveraged advanced AI models for accurate recognition and personalized tour guidance.

## Technologies Used

- **Mobile**: Flutter
- **AI/ML**: Python, Deep Learning, Computer Vision
- **AR**: Unity
- **Cloud**: Google Cloud

## Achievements

- Selected for **Egypt’s GenZ Program** receiving EGP 1.5M funding.
- **Enpact incubation program** in Berlin.
- **Hack To Business 2024** – 1st Place.
